Survival Guide 🧭

Mastering the Art of Staying Alive 🩺

Food & Water 🍽️💧

Starvation and dehydration are silent killers. Check kitchens for canned goods 🥫 or fruit 🍎, but beware—some might be spoiled 🤢. Rivers and wells offer water 💧; boil it with a campfire 🔥 or use purification tablets 💊. Fishing 🎣 by the coast can sustain you if you’ve got a rod and patience.

Combat Basics ⚔️

Fight only when necessary. Melee weapons like axes 🪓 or shovels 🛠️ are quiet and reliable, but firearms 🔫 draw attention. Aim for the head against infected 🧟, and watch for bandits 🏴‍☠️. Ammo is scarce, so make every shot count 🎯.

Navigating Chernarus 🗺️

Key Locations 📍

Chernogorsk 🌊: A bustling coastal city with hospitals 🏥 and apartments 🏢—high loot, higher risk. Northwest Airfield ✈️: Military gear galore 🎖️, but a bandit hotspot 🏴‍☠️. Berezino 🌲: Quieter eastern town with decent supplies 🥫. Green Mountain 📡: Eerie radio tower with rare finds.

Travel Tips 🏃‍♂️

Stick to tree lines 🌳 for cover, avoiding open fields 🌾 where you’re a target 🎯. Roads are fast but exposed—use sparingly 🛤️. A compass 🧭 or map 🗺️ can save you; otherwise, track the sun ☀️. Move at dawn 🌅 for visibility.

Pro Tips for the Wasteland 🌩️

  • Layer up 👕—hypothermia hits fast in rain 🌧️.
  • Stash gear 🎒—bases get raided 🏴‍☠️.
  • Craft a splint 🩹 with sticks 🌿 and rags 👗.
  • Listen for sounds 🔊—gunshots 🔫 mean trouble.
  • Trade with survivors 🤝—beans 🥫 for bullets 🔴.

  • Be Respectful—Treat all players with respect. No harassment, hate speech, racism, or bullying. Keep the wasteland friendly! 🤝

  • Stay On Topic—Use channels for their purpose. DayZ talk, trading, and survival tips go in the right spots. Off-topic? Take it to #general 📍
  • No Spamming—Don’t flood chats with messages, @mentions, emojis, or links. Keep comms clear for scavenging plans 📵
  • No Cheating or Exploits—No hacks, glitches, or third-party bots. Play legit—Beans doesn’t tolerate cheating bandits! ⚖️
  • Voice Chat Etiquette—No screaming, loud noises, or voice changers in voice channels. Use push-to-talk if your mic is noisy 🎙️

  • Respect Privacy—Don’t share personal info about yourself or others. No doxing—keep the apocalypse drama-free 🔒
  • Follow Discord’s Rules—Stick to Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines 📜
  • Report Issues—See a rulebreaker? Ping a mod in #support. Don’t take justice into your own hands, bandit-style 👮
